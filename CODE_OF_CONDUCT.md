# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating empathy and respect
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, intimidation, and personal attacks.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may remove
or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate
this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

Report incidents by opening a private security advisory or by creating an issue
requesting a private contact channel.
